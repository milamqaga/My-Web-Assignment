McDonald's

History: Founded in 1940 by Richard and Maurice McDonald in San Bernardino, California, McDonald’s expanded when Ray Kroc joined in 1954, opening the first franchised location in 1955. Iconic menu items like the Big Mac (1968) and Happy Meal (1979) were introduced. Today, McDonald's is one of the largest global fast food chains, known for burgers, fries, and breakfast items.

Mission & Vision

Mission: McDonald’s aims to be the customer’s favourite fast food destination by providing delicious, feel-good moments, enjoyable dining experiences, and affordable options that are accessible to everyone

Vision: McDonald's focuses on adapting quickly to market changes and customer needs, with a vision for profitable growth, expanding their customer base, and enhancing global accessibility

Target audience

McDonald’s serves a diverse group, including children, teenagers, families, and young professionals, catering to various interests and lifestyles across different age ranges
