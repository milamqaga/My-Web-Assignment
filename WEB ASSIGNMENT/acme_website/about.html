About Acme Community Bakery

History: Founded in 2016 by local bakers, Acme is a community bakery providing handmade bread and pastries while supporting local food programs.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To bake and supply high-quality bread while reinvesting in the local community.

Vision: A connected neighbourhood with access to freshly baked food and vocational training.

Target audience

  • Local residents and families
  • Small cafés and local retailers
  • Event organisers looking for custom cakes
